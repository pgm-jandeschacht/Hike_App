About us

Walk-A-Wish® lets you turn a neighborhood stroll or weekend hike into a fundraiser for good. Just choose a charity and get moving. The app tracks your movement. For every mile you log, you help to earn money for your chosen charity.

Choose from over 100 charities that are making a big impact on health, children, animals, the environment, education, veterans and more.

When you move for charity, you are telling yourself a story about what’s important to you. Side effects may include empathy, mindfulness and gratitude.

We hope you enjoy our app as much as we enjoy offering it to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
